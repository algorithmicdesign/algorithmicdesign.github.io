City Modelling

Generative Design tools facilitate the creation of large-scale 3D models, namely for the representation of both real and fictional cities. These can be used from visualization to city planning.

URBAN ANALYSIS

Buildings are heavily influenced by their urban context. By analyzing not only individual buildings but also their surrounding urban context, we can have a more comprehensive understanding of the relationship between buildings and the city.

ROAD OPTIMIZATION

This work is part of an experiment conducted by our team that aims to reduce the number of roads that need to be built by exploring a physics-inspired optimization algorithm. This algorithm minimizes the total energy of a system of masses and springs simulating roads or traffic.
