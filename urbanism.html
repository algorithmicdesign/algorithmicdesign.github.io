Case Study projects by ADA

Here you can find some of the projects made using our tools and design approach

URBANISM

Generative tools can help us build models of cities faster, taking a 2D map as an input. Regarding city planning, the advantages are also considerable, since urban centers are characterized by patterns and connection networks. Generative tools can help urban planners analyse these patterns and networks, aiding in orderly expansions of the urban fabric, improving arrangements within it, or extrapolating its organizational logic to other cities.

City Modelling

Buildings are heavily influenced by their urban context. By studying not only individual buildings but also their surrounding urban context, we can have a more comprehensive understanding of the relationship between buildings and the city. Using our parametric tools, we are able to not only easily build a 3D model of a real existing city, but also to explore different urban morphologies.

LISBON URBAN FABRIC
Modelling existing cities using a script that takes a 2D map with the respective building heights as an input.
FICTIONAL CITY
A fictional city developed to study the influence of urban structure on solar radiation.

ROAD OPTIMIZATION

This experiment reduces the number of roads that need to be built by exploring a physics-inspired optimization algorithm that minimizes the total energy of a system of masses and springs that simulates roads or traffic.
