PERFORMANCE-BASED DESIGN

Performance-based design is a design process informed by a deeper analysis within the environmental context of a project. By designing according to environmental demands, designers can transition from the traditional process of form making to a more contemporary process of form finding, where more eco-friendly energy- and cost-efficient buildings can be built.

ANALYSIS AND SIMULATION

With the growing demand for sustainable building, it has become crucial for designers to take building performance into account during the development of their projects during early stages design. Analysis tools allow architects to make design decisions informed by building performance, having become important tools for the design process. Using our tools, we seek to make analysis tools easier, faster and more convenient to use.

OPTIMIZATION

Optimization is a field that has been explored recently by more technologically open firms such as Foster and Partners. Nevertheless, the optimization processes used by most only include doing a few iterations of the original design and chose the best performing one. Our approach to optimization uses algorithms, which allows the exploration of more design optimizations, searching for the optimal version of the design amongst many.
