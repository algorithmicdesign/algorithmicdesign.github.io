CORK PANELS FOR CLASSROOM ACOUSTICS

CORK PANELS FOR CLASSROOM ACOUSTICS

Panels Design and Development: ADA
Location: Lisbon
Date: 2017

This project was developed in the context of a design competition, sponsored by Instituto Superior TÃ©cnico, with the aim of improving its academic facilities.
The project proposes the instalation of cork panels inside some of the classrooms, with the goal of improving their poor acoustics. The panels were designed algorithmically and parametrically with Rosetta, allowing us to quickly explore several pattern variations and adapt the panels to the classroom in which they were placed. With the aid of an acoustic analysis software, the panels were algorithmically optimized to the acoustic characteristics of each classrooms.
The design of the panels was inspired by magnetic fields.
