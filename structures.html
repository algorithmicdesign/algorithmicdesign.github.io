Case Study projects by ADA

Here you can find some of the projects made using our tools and design approach.

STRUTURES

Non-planar geometries are increasingly more common in architectural practices nowadays, and designing functional systems to support them is a rising challenge. Generative tools can not only help us plan complex structures, but also optimize them in various ways.

GEOMETRY RATIONALIZATION

Complex geometries are becoming increasingly popular in contemporary architecture. The rationalization of complex surfaces into something that is more suitable for fabrication poses one of the greatest challenges for the construction of these geometries. One of our goals is finding ways to solve these challenges.
