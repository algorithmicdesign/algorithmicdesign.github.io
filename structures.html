Structure Modelling

With generative design tools, it is possible to design and optimize complex structures that would normally require a lot of effort to design using traditional manual approaches. In ADA, we explore these techniques to develop projects more efficiently and materialize our architectural vision.

GEOMETRY PROCESSING

Complex geometries are becoming increasingly popular in contemporary architecture. The rationalization of complex surfaces into something that is more suitable for fabrication poses one of the greatest challenges for the construction of these geometries. One of our goals is finding ways to solve these challenges.
