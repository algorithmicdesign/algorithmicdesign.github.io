Parametric Modeling

In the past years, there has been an increased use of programming tools in architectural design, not only because it allows architects to achieve nouvelle shapes, but also allows them to rapidly experiment and vary their designs. ADA has developed textual generative design tools which allow scaling of architectural designs in a more rapid and efficient way, when compared with visual programming tools. Facade Design

Nowadays, architectural facades are characterized by complex shapes and patterns, mainly due to the use of digital technologies, such as Generative Design, which allows design exploration to go beyond traditional possibilities and human imagination. Using Generative Design, we developed a computational framework for the generation and exploration of facade designs. The framework includes a classification of facades that helps the designer identify the set of algorithms that best suits the needs of their design intent. By combining the various algorithms provided by the framework, the designer can more easily explore the solution space of the intended design. Algorithmic-Based Building Information Modeling (A-BIM)

A-BIM explores the use of algorithmic processes with Building Information Modeling (BIM). This approach offers great advantages to architectural design allowing models to be more flexible while containing building information relevant for construction.
