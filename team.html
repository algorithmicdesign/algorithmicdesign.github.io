Meet our team

The team is composed by architects and computer science engineers,
both practitioners and students in the referred specialities

Senior Researcher

António Menezes Leitão

António Menezes Leitão has a BSc in Mechanical Engineering, a MSc in Electronics Engineering, and a PhD in Computer Science and Engineering, all from Instituto Superior Técnico (IST) of the University of Lisbon. Currently he is Assistant Professor at the same university, senior researcher at INESC-ID, within the Software Engineering Group, and Coordinator of ADA, teaching, lecturing, and researching on bringing together the fields of Computer Science and Architecture.
antonio.menezes.leitao@tecnico.ulisboa.pt

Researcher

Sara Garcia

Sara Garcia holds a Master in Product Design and a PhD in Design, both from the Faculty of Architecture of the University of Lisbon. She is currently collaborating with the Khepri research project, working as a researcher at INESC-ID. Her research interests focus on computational approaches to architecture and design. Particularly, she has been involved in the development of generative systems oriented for design and customization.
sgarcia@fa.ulisboa.pt

Junior Researchers

Catarina Belém

Catarina Belém is a Master's graduate in Computer Science and Engineering from IST, teaching assistant at the same university, and a junior researcher at INESC-ID. Inspired by mathematics, her research focuses on efficient ways of optimizing architectural design problems, typically characterized by time-consuming evaluation functions. Her research also includes applications of Machine Learning to enhance the architectural practice.
catarina.belem@tecnico.ulisboa.pt

Helena Martinho

Helena Martinho is a Master’s graduate in architecture from IST (University of Lisbon) and an intern at INESC-ID. She has also studied at the Technical University of Eindhoven (The Netherlands), focusing on the modeling and simulation of buildings with integrated systems. She is currently researching the development of algorithmic design strategies for dynamic building components.
helena.martinho@tecnico.ulisboa.pt

Inês Caetano

Inês Caetano is an architect graduated at IST (University of Lisbon). She has also studied at Politecnico de Milano (Italy) during her master's. She is currently a junior researcher at INESC-ID, and a PhD student at IST, exploring the integration of algorithmic design methods in the design, analysis and optimization of facade designs.
ines.caetano@tecnico.ulisboa.pt | Portfolio

Inês Pereira

Inês Pereira is a Master's graduate in architecture from Instituto Superior Técnico (University of Lisbon) and an intern at INESC-ID. She is currently researching optimization processes and performance-based approaches to architectural design.
ines.pereira@tecnico.ulisboa.pt

Renata Castelo-Branco

Renata Castelo-Branco is a Master's graduate in architecture from IST, a junior researcher at INESC-ID, and a Computer Science and Engineering PhD student at the same university. She has also studied at the Technical University of Graz (Austria) during her master's. She is currently researching representation methods for algorithmic design.
renata.castelo.branco@tecnico.ulisboa.pt | Portfolio
